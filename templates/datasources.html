Data Sources

Explore the data sources used to identify Type 2 Diabetes (T2D) genetic variants.

Our Key Data Sources

  • Genetic Data from GWAS: Genome-wide association studies (GWAS) provide key insights into genetic variants associated with Type 2 Diabetes.
  • Public Genomic Databases: Data from resources such 1000 Genomes and Type 2 Diabetes Knowledge Portal supported the making of our database.
  • Clinical Data: Patient clinical data, collected from studies and cohorts, enhances our genetic understanding of T2D.
  • Scientific Literature: Peer-reviewed studies provide supporting evidence for genetic associations and variant interpretations.
