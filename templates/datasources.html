Data Sources

Explore the data sources used to identify Type 2 Diabetes (T2D) genetic variants.

Our Key Data Sources

  • 1000 Genomes Project: We downloaded Phase 3 variant VCF files from the 1000 Genomes Project. Genomic data for Punjabi from Lahore, Pakistan and Bengali from Bangladesh populations were extracted for calculating positive selection statistics.
  • T2D KP: Using the Type 2 Diabetes (T2D) Knowledge Portal, we obtained genetic association data for T2D and T2D-related phenotypes.
  • Scientific Literature: The research papers that accompany our genetic association data. [1], [2], [3].
  • PANN-GO Database:We used PAN-GO as a hyperlink for providing gene functional information in the form of Gene Ontology (GO) terms.
  • Ensembl GRCh37: If genes were not found in PAN-GO, Ensembl GRCh37 was used as a fallback for obtaining gene functional information.
