What is PAANN T2D Browser?

PAANN T2D Browser is a tool designed to help researche explore SNPs and genetic variants associated with Type 2 Diabetes (T2D), specific to the South Asian Population. It allows users to easily access information on their SNPs by just typing in their respective RSID, gene name or chromosomal coordinates. The browser also displays a range of preferred statistics available giving the user a comprehensive cover of their SNP of interest.

Features

  • Search SNPs by RSID, gene name, or genomic position
  • View SNP-related annotations and associations
  • Explore linked data sources for deeper insights
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform for genetic researchers studying Type 2 Diabetes. By integrating various data sources, we aim to provide a better understanding of risk factors associated with T2D.

Our Motivations

The motivation behind the development of PAANN T2D Browser is to address the lack of tools that focus on genetic variants associated with Type 2 Diabetes in the South Asian population. Specifically, with such emphasis placed on research for differrent ancestries, we hope to bridge this gape and add to the data pool for this population and contribute to the advancement of genetic research in the field of T2D.
